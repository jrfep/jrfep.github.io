Professional experience
Research Fellow at the Centre for Ecosystem Science, School of Biological, Earth and Environmental Sciences, University of New South Wales (UNSW) from July 2019 to the present. 
Lead the generation of indicative distribution maps for 110 ecosystems functional groups for the publication and report of the IUCN Global Ecosystem Typology, management of code, data and cyberinfrastructure to support the global-ecosystems.org website and database.
Post-doctoral fellow and researcher at Instituto Venezolano de Investigaciones Científicas (IVIC) from 2011 to 2019, and Part-time Research Associate at Provita from 2016 to 2018.
Established the Spatial Ecology Lab at IVIC. Completed and published a continental Red List of Ecosystems assessment for the forests of the Americas
Postdoctoral fellow Threatened Species Programme at Kirstenbosch Research Centre in the South African National Biodiversity Institute (SANBI) and the Botany Department of the University of Cape Town (UCT) from October 2011 to march 2013, and EOL Rubenstein Fellowship Award in 2013.
Worked with biological databases to match species distribution and biotic relationships between butterflies and their host plants.  
Biodiversity Informatics Project Coordinator at the Biodiversity Unit (BiodiVen) at IVIC from 2007 to ­2010.
Coordinated and completed a first national survey of three indicator taxa in Venezuela as part of the Neotropical Biodiversity Mapping Initiative (NeoMaps).
